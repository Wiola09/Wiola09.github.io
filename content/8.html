3 best pubs in Belgrade


Going out in Belgrade is always an exciting experience, and the city has some of the best pubs and clubs in the region. The nightlife here is vibrant and there are many places to choose from. Whether you’re looking for a casual evening with friends or a wild night of dancing, here are my picks for the three best pubs in Belgrade:

1. Beer Garden: This is one of the most popular pubs in Belgrade, and for good reason. It has a great atmosphere, a wide selection of drinks and food, and a large outdoor space to enjoy the warm summer nights. It’s also full of hot guys, so if you’re looking for a date, it’s the perfect place to find one.

2. The White Horse: This pub is the perfect spot for a romantic dinner. Its elegant atmosphere is perfect for a date night, and the food is delicious. Plus, they have a great selection of beers and cocktails.

3. Club 20/44: This is the ultimate spot for a night of partying. The club is always full of people and the music is always pumping. Plus, they have an amazing selection of drinks and a variety of different dance floors to choose from.

These are my picks for the three best places for going out in Belgrade. Whether you’re looking for a casual evening with friends or an all-out night of partying, these pubs and clubs are sure to provide the perfect atmosphere.
